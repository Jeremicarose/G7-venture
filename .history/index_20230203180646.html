Our Story.

We were established in response to the challenges young African web3 entrepreneurs have in accessing funding for the development and scaling of their innovative web3 solutions. We aim to democratise access to funding by making it smooth and simple to regardless of where in Africa the founders may be from At 7G Ventures, we believe that the African web3 ecosystem can significantly thrive and address the majority of its pressing issues if given a level playing field, the attention it requires and the resources it needs.

What we do

Funding- We collaborate with partners to give web3 entrepreneurs financial help as they develop innovative web3 solutions.

Industry partnerships- We progress in identifying areas of synergy and collaboration between us and other companies for the benefit of our portfolio companies.

Consulting- In order to examine the ideal areas of solution deployment, Use cases, and technical execution, we consult for both private and public sector enterprises.

Capacity building and awareness- We believe that an individual is only going to utilise the technology that he understands. That is why we devote the majority of our time and resources to raising awareness about the potential of next-generation technology
